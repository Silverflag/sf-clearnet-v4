About | Our Mission

We are a group of people that are working to provide information anonymously.


We plan to follow these steps to be able to get better anonymous information access for everyone.

-Run static mirrors of services like Wikipedia that can be accessed anonymously.

-Provide a collection of RSS streams that can be accessed anonymously on our server.

-Provide curated articles of the most important updates that can be accessed anonymously.


We plan to follow these concepts to keep the legality of our site in a better state to prevent issues.

-Make sure that there is punishment for misusing an anonymizing tool.

-Make sure that we keep up to all standards of user data privacy (prefferably not having any user data at all on our servers)


I think it is important to clarify what I mean by the "punishment for misuse"

Tor has gotten a bad reputation over the years from people using it to do illegal or generally bad actions.

We want a way for people to have a legal & well respected way of accessing or sharing information anonymously.
